# Phishing Email Analysis Report

## Analyst
Cyber Security Intern

## Objective
To identify phishing indicators present in a suspicious email sample.

---

## Email Details

| Field | Value |
|-------|--------|
| Sender | support@sbi-onlineverification.in |
| Subject | URGENT: Your SBI Account Has Been Temporarily Blocked |
| Organization Impersonated | State Bank of India (SBI) |
| Attack Type | Phishing |

---

## Analysis Findings

### 1. Suspicious Sender Address
The sender domain attempts to impersonate the official State Bank of India domain.

### 2. Urgent Language
The email attempts to create panic by claiming account suspension.

### 3. Threatening Message
The email threatens permanent account suspension within 12 hours.

### 4. Fake URL
The provided URL is not an official SBI domain.

### 5. Generic Greeting
The sender uses "Dear Customer" rather than the recipient's actual name.

### 6. Social Engineering
The attacker exploits fear and urgency to manipulate the victim.

### 7. Email Spoofing
The email impersonates a trusted organization to gain credibility.

### 8. Header Analysis
Header inspection indicates possible SPF, DKIM, and DMARC authentication failures.

## Risk Assessment

| Category | Risk Level |
|----------|-----------|
| Credential Theft | High |
| Financial Fraud | High |
| Malware Risk | Medium |

---

## Recommended Actions

- Avoid clicking suspicious links.
- Verify sender authenticity.
- Report phishing emails.
- Enable multi-factor authentication.
- Use updated antivirus software.

---

## Conclusion

This email exhibits multiple phishing characteristics and should be considered malicious. Users should avoid interacting with the email and report it immediately.
